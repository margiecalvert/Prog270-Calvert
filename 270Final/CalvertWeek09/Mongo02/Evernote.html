About Evernote and Mongo

Evernote

This image shows Evernote running in the Asus ME173X. Evernote gives the user the capability of making notes and storing them in the cloud. This is very useful and makes the phone an access point for storing and retrieving data. It is great for shopping lists and information about products that you are comparing.

It is possible to search through all the documents in Evernote.

Evernote syncs through the Internet. This page shows the time of the last sync.
