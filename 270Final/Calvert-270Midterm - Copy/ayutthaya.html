Ayutthaya

Ayutthaya Historical Park is about 50 kilometers from Bangkok, and can be reached by bus or train. It can also be reached by river boat from Bangkok. Because the site is large, most visitors sign up for a guided tour, or else rent a bike. It is possible to ride an elephant here as well. Here are some pictures of Ayutthaya today.
